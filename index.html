    About Me

    I’m an Electrical Engineering Fresh Graduated of Sebelas Maret University. I have some experience like Organization Experience, Job Experience, and Project Experience. The Experience was obtained during my college. I’m discipline, able to adapth and fast learner with good analysis and problem solving. I’m also able to work in any condition in team or individualy. Not afraid of challenges and optimism for what is being done and I have a good interpersonal skills.

    During college, apart studying strong currents and weak currents, I also studied about Computer System (Web and Software Development, Big Data, Meachine Learning, Internet of Things, and Artificial Intelligence) and Telecommunication System (Satelite and mobile Communication System, Antenna and Wave Propagation, and Remote Sensing). In my final project, I made a Design S-Band Antenna Microstrip with Circular Polarized for Synthetic Aperture Radar (CP-SAR) Sensors.
