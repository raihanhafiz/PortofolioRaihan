- Portofolio-

Design Of S-Band Microstrip Antenna With Circular Polarized For Synthetic Aperture Radar (CP-SAR) Sensors

The design and simulation process uses CST Studio Suite software with the FR-4 substrate material, with a dielectric constant of 4.3 at a frequency of 2.25 GHz. The design and simulation results show that the bandwidth in the simulation is 141 MHz, and the measurement is 100 MHz; the VSWR value in the simulation is 1.2. The measurement is 1.35, the axial ratio bandwidth is 264 MHz, the gain is 3.82 dBi, and the antenna's impedance during the simulation is 62 Ω and measurement of 43 Ω.

Design of the Smartphone IoT-based Non-Invasive Glucose Urine Detector for an Early Detection of Diabetes

Recently, diabetes mellitus has received significant attention due to their estimation that of the 382 million people, 175 million of them have not been diagnosed. One method for diagnosing diabetes is to use non-invasive methods, by taking fluids in the body in the form of urine. The method does not cause pain in the patient's body and avoids psychological effects on patients who fear needles. This study has been designed using fixed potential amperometry. The design of the tool starts from the fabrication of the Arduino-based potentiostat by utilizing the ADC, DAC, summing amplifier, and trans-impedance amplifier, which will be used for electrochemical analysis urine samples. The output obtained will be displayed on the OLED screen so that users can see the test results directly, accurately, and pain-free. This tool was further developed by adding features connected to an Android Smartphone via IoT and adding features for patient medical record data. This design can help avoid patients suffering from diabetes.
